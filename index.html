Good to know

Questions, answered

Where are FILTER's salons in Abu Dhabi?
We have two branches: Al Raha (Al Faridah Complex) and Al Bateen (Marasy, Building 4). Guests visit us from across Abu Dhabi — Yas Island, Khalifa City, Khalidiya and the Corniche.
Can I book the salon privately?
Yes — VIP clients can reserve an entire branch exclusively for bridal parties, celebrations or private appointments, subject to availability and VIP tier pricing. Enquire on WhatsApp.
What are your opening hours?
Both salons open daily 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM, with late evenings until 10:00 PM on Tuesdays and Fridays.
Which brands do you work with?
Kérastase, K18, Schwarzkopf, L'Oréal Professionnel, Authentic Beauty Concept, HD Brows and Nouveau Lashes.
